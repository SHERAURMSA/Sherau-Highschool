Our Commitment to Education

The Government Secondary and Higher Secondary School, SHERAU, serves the community of Tharad, Banaskantha, with a dedication to accessible and quality education. Established to uplift the academic standards in the region, we provide comprehensive education from Secondary to Higher Secondary levels in various streams.

  • Government-accredited curriculum
  • Dedicated Science and General Streams
  • Focus on Board Exam Excellence
